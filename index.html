Bio

Jiyuan Liu is currently a PhD student in National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), China. His research interests include multi-view clustering, deep clustering and anomaly detection. Jiyuan Liu has published papers in highly regarded journals and conferences such as IEEE T-KDE, AAAI, IJCAI, etc. He serves as Program Committee member and reviewer on highly regarded journals and conferences, such as TNNLS, AAAI21, IJCAI21 (SPC), etc.
